    What is ICS?

    This website is directed towards UTeM students and staff, where they can receive all information about UTeM which includes each faculty and dorm. Students can see the information about their faculty and keep up-to-date. On this website , student are able to surf and also lodge a complaint about UTeM.


    How the complaint system works?

    The Complaint system is divided into two users; Students and staff. We expect those whosign in, to choose between the students and staff section accordingly. Users may fill in the form and submit to file a complaint. This system will alert University Authorities to take action from your complaint.
