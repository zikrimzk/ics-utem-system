Faculty of Electronic & Computer Engineering

FKEKK… where engineering meets the future

FKEKK offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs. Established in 2000, FKEKK provides a sound foundation for students to transition from higher education into the workforce. Students learn from experienced lecturers and industry experts.

A career in engineering

FKEKK offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs. It specializes in electronic engineering and computer engineering fields. Explore the possibilities of your future with a Bachelor’s Degree in Electronic Engineering or Computer Engineering. All programs in FKEKK are accredited by Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA) and Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM).

Become a leader in your field

Become a leader in the world of electronics and computer engineering. With FKEKK, you can access postgraduate programs in electronic engineering through our industry-centric curriculum and top researchers as educators.

Build your portfolio

Qualified engineering graduates in less than 3 years

The Diploma in Electronic Engineering is a two-and-a-half-year, full-time program that will equip you with professional knowledge and skills. You’ll be able to work in the industry immediately after graduation – making use of your diploma and BEM accredited status.

“
Get your degree in one of on-demand engineering field!

Learn how to develop and design electronic components and circuits. Explore the latest technologies, from hardware to software, and master skills in embedded systems programming, digital signal processing, and microcontrollers. Gain a robust foundation in electronics engineering principles, techniques, and practices.

Associate Profesor Dr Masrullizam Mat Ibrahim,
Dean, FKEKK
